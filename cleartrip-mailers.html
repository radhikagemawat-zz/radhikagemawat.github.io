ABOUT

Cleartrip is India's second largest travel agency. During my internship, I worked on the project which entailed about cracking a template for the emailers sent to the customers for primary booking of air and hotel. The existing system was becoming obsolete, primarily because the content got outdated and wasn't responsive.


INTRODUCTION OF PROJECT

The project was named as "Postman". My mentor, Kedar introduced me to two types of mailers in the beginning:

  • High impact mailers: domestic confirmation- hotel, flight, train; complete cancellation mailers; book shortlisted hotels
  • Delight mailers: first booking, continue booking where you left, fare alerts

We were particularly asked to focus on high impact mailers for the initiation of the project.

Principle: 3Cs- Crisp, Consistent, Common Template


RESEARCH

Altering the complete template for the mails of company, we were required to understand the high quality and regard for the product. We were provided with the complete database of the mailers the company entailed. Gradually, I prepared cases and tried to understand the product length and breadth.


AGENDA OF PROJECT

  • Consistent look and feel of all the emailers
  • All emailers to be responsive for most used mobile email clients
  • All emailers to have a fallback as pure text emailrs
  • All emailers to have contextual / consistent upsell / promotion driven by a good logic
  • Personalised communication for different types of Cleartrip isers
  • Design as a solution to improve the speed of maintenance of these emailers (for future change)
  • Logic of serving ads in the emailers


GOALS

  • Design: Meaningful conversation, responsive behaviour
  • Product: Accurate SLAs, handling silly emails like xero cancelation fee, finalise business and marketting requirements
  • Developmenet: Migration to a more structured code

We were expected to work across all the three dimensions of the product i.e. design sensibly, understand market dynamics of product and most importantly integrate the mailers with the dev guys.

UNDERSTANDING BRANDING AND GUIDELINES

The design team was very particular about each and every pixel, and functionality was of key importance. Getting to work with such a skilled team instilled upon me a sense of responsibility and sensitivity towards my behaviour as a product designer.

Meanwhile we were introduced to the design parameters of company; which were exclusive to branding and guidelines.


Typography guidelines:


- No Capslock sentences or words ever
- Never user The Sans for paragraphs (short or long)
- Never user The Sans for numbers. Always use helvetica
- Never user The Sans smaller than 30px. If required, change it to Helvetica.
