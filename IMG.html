ABOUT

IMG stands as the official technical group of college campus which as a group of 40 tech enthusiasts manage the main website and the intranet portal. The portfolio represents the work culture and projects completed by the group, and the far-reaching impact on the campus' students and staff.


HISTORY

The first version of portfolio was a static website, which entailed blogs, projects, about team and team members' names. Functionally, the website configured all the features a portfolio website should have. However being static, it posed the following problems: being static, it didn't facilitate a user friendly experience for the members of the team primarily because it didn't let them update content (the rights rested with super-admin) like: blogs, projects, team members.
Why we need a new website:
- Interface of admin portal of current website is not good
- There is no hierarchy in the order of the tabs
- No mention of our Alumni, through which we can let others know of our strong alumni base
- The website does not have a proper timeline
V2 features
The version.2 was based on a dyamic django framwwork, with additional features which entailed a trending block (updates about IMG and members).

personas

two different personas :

  • general users: the general users can only view the projects, blogs and members' profile of the team
  • team members: team members have the rights to publish their own blogs, projects and update their own profile

PAPER WIREFRAMING


Flow was decided for the website in a manner wherein for team members, additional buttons were incorporated; minimising the alterations in user flow for the personas. The user flow drawn depicts all the points of interactions. Tabs were decided as follows:

  • Projects
  • Blogs
  • Team
  • Alumni

Eventually, I began working on the mock-ups of the website and the team alongside beagn the development across the back-end as well as front-end. Once the Urls were decided, backend was completed within few weeks.
Special priviledges:
Super-admin Portal (all senior students) and alumni ) - Profile (make any changes if required) -update profile- Blog/Projects -write or edit any blog/project
